Potential Risks of Unsecured Wi-Fi

  • Data Theft: Hackers can intercept sensitive data such as passwords and credit card details.
  • Unauthorized Access: Malicious users may exploit your Wi-Fi to access your connected devices.
  • Illegal Activities: Your network could be used for unlawful activities, making you liable.
  • Malware Injection: Cybercriminals can introduce malware or ransomware into your devices.
  • Network Monitoring: An attacker could track your browsing habits and compromise your privacy.
